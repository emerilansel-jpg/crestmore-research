What We Offer

Our Services

Six integrated service lines — from continuous market intelligence to bespoke commissioned research — designed for the demands of institutional decision-making.

Market Intelligence

Continuous coverage of the markets that matter to you.

Our flagship market intelligence service provides institutional clients with continuous, forward-looking coverage across commodity markets, capital flows, and emerging market dynamics.

Each coverage area is assigned a dedicated senior analyst supported by a regional research network. Clients receive a weekly intelligence bulletin, a monthly in-depth report, and on-demand analyst briefings.

Key Deliverables

  • Weekly intelligence bulletins (3–5 pages)
  • Monthly deep-dive research reports (30–60 pages)
  • Quarterly market outlook documents
  • Real-time data alerts via our client portal
  • Unlimited analyst briefing calls

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • Base Metals & Mining
  • Precious Metals
  • Energy Transition Minerals
  • Agricultural Commodities
  • Freight & Logistics

Due Diligence Support

Research built to withstand investment committee scrutiny.

Crestmore provides structured due diligence research for investment teams evaluating transactions in commodity-linked sectors and emerging markets. Our work supports M&A processes, private equity investments, infrastructure financing, and project finance decisions.

We operate under NDA and work within your timeline. Our team has experience supporting transactions ranging from $50M to over $5Bn in enterprise value.

Key Deliverables

  • Market and competitive landscape assessment
  • Commodity price and demand scenario modelling
  • Regulatory and country risk assessment
  • Supply chain and logistics analysis
  • Management presentation support

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • Private Equity
  • Infrastructure Funds
  • Corporate M&A
  • Development Finance
  • Project Finance

Country Risk Assessment

47 markets. Specialist coverage. No generic scorecards.

Generic country risk scores mask the nuance that drives real investment outcomes. Crestmore's country risk assessments are built from primary research, structured interviews with government officials and industry participants, and deep institutional knowledge of how each market actually operates.

We cover 47 countries across Sub-Saharan Africa, Latin America, MENA, Central Asia, and Southeast Asia — in meaningful depth, not superficially.

Key Deliverables

  • Sovereign risk profiles (political, fiscal, regulatory)
  • Sector-specific regulatory risk assessments
  • Expropriation and contract enforcement analysis
  • Currency and capital controls assessment
  • Quarterly risk rating updates with change rationale

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • Mining & Extractives
  • Infrastructure
  • Agriculture
  • Manufacturing
  • Financial Services

Sector Deep-Dives

Specialist analysis you cannot get from generalist providers.

Our sector deep-dives provide the kind of granular, technically-grounded analysis that generalist research houses cannot replicate. Each report is anchored in primary data collection, often including field visits, operator interviews, and trade data analysis.

Published on a scheduled basis, our sector reports function as definitive reference documents for institutional investors and corporate strategists.

Key Deliverables

  • Annual sector outlook reports (80–120 pages)
  • Supply-demand modelling to 10-year horizon
  • Price forecasting with scenario analysis
  • Competitive landscape mapping
  • Key risk and opportunity matrices

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • Copper & Critical Minerals
  • Gold & Silver
  • Iron Ore & Steel
  • Lithium & Battery Materials
  • Fertilisers & Agri-inputs

Advisory Retainers

A Crestmore analyst on your team — without the overhead.

Our retainer clients receive priority access to Crestmore analysts, our proprietary datasets, and the full body of published research. Retainers are structured as ongoing relationships, typically for 12-month periods, enabling us to provide contextualised analysis that deepens over time.

This is our most valued service tier. We work with a limited number of retainer clients to ensure quality is maintained.

Key Deliverables

  • Unlimited analyst Q&A sessions (phone, video, in-person)
  • Full access to all published research and data
  • Custom research requests (up to 4 per quarter)
  • Priority turnaround on time-sensitive queries
  • Annual in-person strategy review session

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • Sovereign Wealth Funds
  • Pension Funds
  • Commodity Trading Houses
  • Corporate Strategy Teams
  • Family Offices

Custom Research

Your specific question. Our dedicated research team.

For research needs that fall outside our standard service lines, we offer bespoke commissioned research. From a rapid 48-hour briefing note to a multi-month deep-dive programme, we tailor our approach to your timeline, budget, and deliverable requirements.

Custom research mandates are subject to availability and a brief scoping conversation with our senior team.

Key Deliverables

  • Rapid briefing notes (24–72 hour turnaround)
  • Structured research reports to client specification
  • Data builds and proprietary database construction
  • Expert network interviews and synthesis
  • Presentation decks for board or IC use

Typical Clients / Sectors

  • All sectors under active Crestmore coverage
  • Adjacent sectors on a case-by-case basis
